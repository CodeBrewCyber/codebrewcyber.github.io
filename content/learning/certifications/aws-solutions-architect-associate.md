## Overview

The AWS Certified Solutions Architect – Associate (**SAA-C03**) validates the ability to design secure, resilient, high-performing, and cost-optimized architectures on AWS. It's the most popular AWS associate certification and a strong signal of practical cloud fluency — core AWS services, the Well-Architected Framework, networking, and security all feature heavily.

**Status:** Earned

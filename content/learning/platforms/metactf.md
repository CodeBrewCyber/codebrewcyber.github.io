## Overview

MetaCTF hosts Capture the Flag competitions and maintains a challenge library available year-round. It's well-suited for individuals building CTF fundamentals as well as organizations running internal or competitive team events. Challenges span web, binary exploitation, forensics, cryptography, and OSINT.

## Details

- **Cost:** Free for individual challenges; event hosting has pricing tiers
- **Skill Level:** Beginner → Intermediate
- **Focus:** CTF competitions, year-round practice challenges, team events
- **Link:** [metactf.com](https://metactf.com)

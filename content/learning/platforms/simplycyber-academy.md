## Overview

SimplyCyber Academy is built by Gerald Auger (PhD, CISSP) and is aimed at people actively pursuing a cybersecurity career. The content leans heavily toward GRC (Governance, Risk, and Compliance), blue team operations, and the soft skills needed to land and grow in a security role. It's a strong complement to purely technical platforms for those targeting analyst or governance positions.

## Details

- **Cost:** Mix of free and paid courses
- **Skill Level:** Beginner → Intermediate
- **Focus:** GRC, blue team, career development, security analyst fundamentals
- **Link:** [simplycyber.io/academy](https://www.simplycyber.io/academy)
